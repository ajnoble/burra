Dialog Behaviors

  • Approve — optional note to member, changes PENDING → CONFIRMED, sends approval email with pay link
  • Cancel — shows policy-calculated refund, admin can override amount, requires reason, releases beds
  • Cancel (unpaid booking) — refund section hidden, just reason + confirm
  • Reassign Beds — inline dropdowns filtered to available beds for the booking's date range
  • Bed dropdowns — only show beds not booked by other guests for those dates
